# CodeQuest Certification Framework

CodeQuest credentials are internal learning credentials. They are not externally accredited
qualifications unless a future accredited partner explicitly validates them.

Credential eligibility uses multiple evidence types:

- academy progress;
- practical projects;
- guided capstones;
- technical viva;
- workplace missions;
- production releases.

Browser exam integrity signals are advisory only. Window changes, completion speed and answer
changes do not prove misconduct. Any consequential decision requires human review.

Written questions are scored with transparent keyword coverage in v50. This is a learning
assessment mechanism, not equivalent to expert human marking.
