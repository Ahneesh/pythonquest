# CodeQuest Public Beta Launch Checklist

Do not add another major product feature until the required launch gates are tested.

Required evidence:

- Playwright critical journeys pass.
- Email and Google sign-in work.
- Academy selection remains correct after refresh.
- Textbook, audio, exercise and mastery work together.
- Progress is restored in another browser.
- At least one complete project works.
- Stripe test-mode Starter, Monthly and Annual journeys pass.
- Supabase RLS and server entitlements are verified.
- Primary navigation has no dead items.
- Mobile core journey is usable.
- No uncaught errors occur in critical journeys.
- Problem reporting is available.
- Privacy, terms, pricing and cancellation are clear.

Recommended first beta cohort: 10–25 learners.
