# CodeQuest Freemium Product Model

Commercial plans:

- Free
- 15-Day Starter Pack
- Pro Monthly
- Pro Annual

There is no cohort or team subscription. Team Engineering is a Pro learning feature.

The Starter Pack is a one-off purchase and must not renew automatically.

Subscription state must ultimately be authoritative on the server. Browser/local state in this
release is for product-flow testing only.
