# CodeQuest Team Engineering Standard

Team projects should demonstrate:

1. A defined team role.
2. A visible sprint backlog.
3. Work performed on a named branch.
4. A pull request with a clear description.
5. Specific peer-review comments.
6. A review decision.
7. A merged contribution or documented revision.
8. A sprint reflection.

Peer-review evidence should reward useful engineering judgement rather than comment volume.
