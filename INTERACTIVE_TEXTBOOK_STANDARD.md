# CodeQuest Interactive Textbook Standard

Every lesson must contain:

1. Learning objectives
2. Concept overview
3. Why it matters
4. Mental model
5. Mechanics
6. Syntax anatomy
7. Worked example
8. Second example
9. Common mistakes
10. Debugging guide
11. Workplace application
12. Knowledge check
13. Glossary
14. Summary

Coverage in v62:

- Python: 59 chapters
- SQL: 20 chapters
- Web: 20 chapters
- Java: 20 chapters

The short five-page theory carousel remains available as Quick Review. It is not a replacement
for the full textbook chapter.
