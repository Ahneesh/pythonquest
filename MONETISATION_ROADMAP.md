# CodeQuest monetisation roadmap

No payments are enabled in v46.

Potential future tiers:

## Free

- Core academy lessons
- Limited coding practice
- Basic progress tracking
- One portfolio

## Pro learner

- Advanced missions
- Unlimited AI mentor usage
- Job matching and application packs
- Verified credentials
- Premium portfolio exports
- Interview analytics

## Instructor / cohort

- Cohort management
- Assignments and grading
- Learner analytics
- Curriculum Studio
- Team and hackathon management

## Employer

- Talent discovery
- Verified evidence profiles
- Sponsored missions
- Candidate shortlisting
- Skills analytics

Any commercial implementation should define pricing, refunds, taxes, billing terms and data
processing before payment functionality is enabled.
